Our Values, Our Strengths, and Our Team

Our Values

Reliability

We guarantee the reliability of our products, our team, and our commitments. Consistent performance and rigorous organization ensure your satisfaction and a strong partnership.

Proactive Improvement

We believe in active listening and continuous adaptation. By integrating your feedback, we anticipate your needs and constantly improve our solutions to stay at the forefront of innovation.

Eco-Responsibility

We are committed to reducing our ecological footprint by producing durable batteries and adopting environmentally friendly practices for a greener future.

Our Strengths

Local Expertise and Warranty

Our batteries are assembled in Madagascar by a trained and dedicated team. This local expertise allows us to maintain a high level of quality while actively contributing to the country's industrial development. Additionally, our Malagasy warranty ensures fast and efficient after-sales service.

Dedicated Customer Support

Our commitment doesn’t end with the sale. We remain available after installation to ensure the proper functioning of our batteries, answer technical questions, and intervene quickly when needed.

Component Reliability

We select high-quality LiFePO₄ cells from certified suppliers. This commitment guarantees our batteries a long lifespan, thermal stability, and consistent performance.
