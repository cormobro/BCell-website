High-Quality LiFePO4 Cells

All BCELL batteries are assembled using CE-certified A+ prismatic cells from a world-renowned supplier.

  • Up to 12,000 cycles at 80% depth of discharge (0.5C).
  • Continuous power 0.5C and 200A peak.
  • Resistant to temperature fluctuations, shocks, and vibrations.
  • No cell deformation, even under intensive use.
  • Perfect balancing thanks to precise cell pairing.

Result: Longevity, reliability, and consistent performance for all your projects.

Battery Management System (BMS)

The BMS is the brain of the battery: performance, safety, and continuous optimization.

  • Real-time monitoring of critical parameters.
  • Automatic cell balancing.
  • Management of protection components and data logging.
  • Preventive activation of safety mechanisms.
  • Parameter adaptation to the environment.

Beyond control, the BMS ensures active safety and prepares for future optimization.
